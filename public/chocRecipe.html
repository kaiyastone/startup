    • Choclate Chip Cookie Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 egg
    • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 ts baking soda
    • 1/2 ts salt
    • 1 cup chocolate chips

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 F
    2. in large bowl, mix butter with the sugars until well-combined
    3. stir in vanilla and egg
    4. add flour, baking soda, and salt
    5. mix dough until just combined
    6. stir in chocolate chips
    7. place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet in about 1.5 TB portions
    8. bake for 7-10 minutes
