    • Cinnamon Swirl Cookie Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 1 cups butter (softened) -> dough
    • 1/2 cup butter -> cinnamon sugar mixture
    • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
    • 1 cup brown sugar
    • 2 ts vanilla extract -> dough
    • 1 ts vanilla extract -> frosting
    • 3 egg
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 ts baking soda
    • 1 ts salt
    • 4 ts ground cinnamon
    • 4 oz cream cheese
    • 2 TB milk
    • 4 cups powdered sugar

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 F
    2. in large bowl, mix butter with the white sugar until well-combined
    3. beat in vanilla and egg
    4. add flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt
    5. mix dough until just combined
    6. stir in chocolate chips
    7. place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet in large portions
    8. bake for 10-12 minutes
    9. in separate bowl cinnamon sugar topping, combine melted butter, cinnamon, and brown sugar
    10. spoon about 2 ts of the mixture over each cookie and let cool
    11. in another bowl for the frosting, beat the cream cheese and butter until creamy and smooth
    12. add the milk, vanilla, and salt, then beat again
    13. transfer to piping bag and pipe in swirls onto each cookie
