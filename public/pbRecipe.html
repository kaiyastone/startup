    • Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 cups flour
    • 1/2 cup butter
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    • 1 cup peanut butter
    • 1 ts vanilla extract
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1 egg
    • 3/4 tsp baking powder

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 F
    2. sift flour and baking powder together then whisk to combine
    3. cream butter and sugars in a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment
    4. add peanut butter and mix until incorporated
    5. mix in egg and vanilla extract then add flour mixture and beat until incorporated
    6. roll dough into 1 inch balls and place on baking sheet
    7. flatten cookies with a fork in a criss-cross pattern
    8. bake for about 10 minutes
